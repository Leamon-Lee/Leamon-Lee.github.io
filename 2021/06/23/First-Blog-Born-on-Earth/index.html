我的第一个博客诞生了！—— First Blog Born on Earth

Posted on 2021-06-23 Edited on 2021-08-21 Views: Changyan:

  一个闷骚的高中技术宅在家里搞出了他人生中的第一个博客网站。这个网站是在新冠疫情爆发前所想建的，但因种种原因，迟迟不能落实。

  直到2021年的6月后旬，在初中毕业后的暑假才开逐步学习Hexo和Markdown语言，结果没想到的是，Hexo建博客没有花费我大量的时间与经历，于7月初就落实建成现在你能看到的一个粗糙的网站。这是我的第一次尝试去学写博客文章。

  This website was intended to be built before COVID-19 broke out, but for various reasons, it could not be implemented.

  It wasn’t until the last ten days of June 2021 that I started learning Hexo and Markdown gradually in the summer vacation after graduating from junior high school.

  Unexpectedly, it didn’t take me a lot of time and experience to build Hexo blog. So, I built a rough website in early July, which you can see now.
